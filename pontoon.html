I, on behalf of myself, my guests, heirs, and legal representatives, hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue Most Litt Party Rides, its owners, captains, crew, employees, and agents from all claims arising from bodily injury, death, or property damage sustained during the pontoon boat rental, even if caused by the negligence of the Released Parties.

3. CAPTAIN'S AUTHORITY. The captain has full and final authority over all vessel operations and passenger conduct. All passengers must follow the captain's instructions at all times. Failure to comply with crew instructions is grounds for immediate return to dock with no refund.

4. PASSENGER CONDUCT. I agree to be responsible for the conduct of all guests in my party. All passengers must remain within the vessel boundaries while the boat is in motion. No guest may operate the vessel without explicit captain authorization. Disorderly, aggressive, or unsafe behavior will result in immediate termination of service.

5. SWIMMING & WATER ENTRY. Swimming is only permitted at designated stops and with captain approval. All swimmers must wear a life jacket unless they have demonstrated swimming ability to the captain's satisfaction. No jumping from the roof or elevated surfaces of the vessel. Diving is prohibited.

6. ALCOHOL POLICY. Alcohol consumption is permitted for guests 21+ in compliance with local laws. I agree that intoxicated passengers will not be permitted to swim and must remain seated. The captain reserves the right to refuse service to visibly intoxicated individuals and return the vessel to dock if alcohol is causing safety concerns.

7. CHILDREN & MINORS. All minors must wear properly fitted life jackets at all times while on board. Adults are responsible for supervising minors in their care. I certify that all minors are accompanied by a parent or legal guardian who has agreed to these terms.

8. VESSEL DAMAGE & CLEANLINESS. I accept responsibility for any damage to the vessel, equipment, or property caused by myself or my guests beyond normal wear. I agree to return the vessel in the same clean condition it was received. Excessive cleaning fees may be charged for violations.

9. WEATHER & SAFETY CANCELLATION. The captain has sole authority to cancel, shorten, or alter the cruise route due to unsafe weather, mechanical issues, or other safety concerns. Full refunds or reschedules will be offered for captain-initiated cancellations.

10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT. I confirm that I am 18 years of age or older, have read this complete waiver, understand all terms, and voluntarily agree to be legally bound by its provisions on behalf of myself and all guests in my party.
